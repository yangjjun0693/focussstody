General

Focussstody is a study & focus timer built by a solo developer that may improve you focusing. Every minute you study earns you $STUDY token, XP, and helps you climb the leaderboard. It also has a live study room where you can see other people studying in real time.
Yes, completely free. All core features — timer, leaderboard, friends, daily challenges, study room — are free forever. Pro unlocks a few cosmetic extras like exclusive themes and CSV export.
You can sign up via the sign up page. Just enter your email and create a password. No phone number or social media login required.

Tokens & Ranks

$STUDY tokens are your in-app currency earned by studying. They're not real money — they're a gamification mechanic. You can spend them in the Store on themes, tags, and other cosmetic items.
Ranks are based on your total study time (XP). Starting from Apprentice, you level up through Scholar, Analyst, Researcher, and finally Grand Master. The progress bar at the bottom shows how close you are to the next rank.

Pro & Donations

Hit the ₿ Donate button in the timer page, send any amount via crypto or Korean Bank if you're Korean, then DM us on Instagram (@focussstody_official) after donating. We'll upgrade your account to Pro.
